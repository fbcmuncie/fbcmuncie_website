Updates in light of

COVID-19

In this unprecedented time, we are working hard to keep the First Baptist family safe and loved. Here are several ways that you can be involved during this season of social distancing.

Phone Calls

We encourage you to call and check on those that you know and love. A team of people from First Baptist is calling shut-ins and active members each week to check on them, inform them of updates, and pray with them.

YouTube Channel

Videos are being provided each week on the church YouTube channel. Sermons, music, children's Sunday school lessons and more. Be sure to subscribe for up to date information.

Facebook Live Prayer

Each Wednesday at 7 PM, join us for virtual prayer group on the church Facebook page.

Blog Updates

All CCT updates during this season will be posted on the blog.
